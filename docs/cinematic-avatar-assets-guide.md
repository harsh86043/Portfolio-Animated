# Cinematic Background Avatar Frame Assets Guide

This guide outlines the custom image and video generation prompts specifically tailored for your **cinematic background avatar frame system**, utilizing your real photos from Google Drive as the facial reference.

These prompts are designed to preserve your exact identity (face shape, jawline, beard and mustache pattern, clear rectangular eyeglasses, hair styled in a thick black swept-up quiff) while painting you into dark, futuristic, high-contrast background scenes with clean safe zones for website content.

## 🎬 Option B: Video-First Workflow Prompt (Sora / Runway Gen-3)

If you prefer to generate a single continuous transition video and extract keyframes, use this highly engineered prompt in a video generator (like Runway Gen-3 Alpha, Sora, or Luma Dream Machine).

### 16:9 Continuous Scroll Video Prompt
> **Prompt:** A premium 16:9 cinematic website background video. The camera slowly tracks horizontally and pushes in through a series of ultra-high-end dark digital environments with seamless transitions. The video features one consistent character throughout: a 28-year-old Indian male with thick, textured dark black hair styled in a modern high-volume swept-up quiff, wearing clean transparent clear-frame rectangular spectacles, and having a neatly groomed light stubble beard and mustache with a sharp jawline. 
> 
> * **Phase 1 (0-3s - Hero):** He stands on the far right of a dark cyberpunk cityscape at twilight, looking forward confidently with neon orange edge rim lighting. The left side is clean dark negative space with purple mist.
> * **Phase 2 (3-6s - About):** Smooth camera drift transitions to him sitting on the right in a thoughtful, focused profile pose, looking over his shoulder toward a floating glowing terminal. Left side is clean violet space.
> * **Phase 3 (6-9s - Skills):** A cinematic glitch-swipe transition places him on the far left, turned to the right gesturing confidently. The right side is dark negative space with a glowing blue abstract cyber grid.
> * **Phase 4 (9-12s - Projects):** Transition to him on the far right in a high-tech control room looking at glowing diagnostics. Left side is a deep blue empty cyber grid.
> * **Phase 5 (12-15s - Experience):** Transition to him on the far left wearing a tailored dark futuristic executive high-collar coat, looking calm, mature, and professional. Right side is clean dark corporate charcoal space.
> * **Phase 6 (15-18s - Contact):** Camera pans to him on the right side, smiling warmly and looking at the viewer from a futuristic terminal desk. Left side is clean warm amber-orange twilight gradient space.
> 
> * **Stylistic Instructions:** Ultra-realistic, 8k, photorealistic, volumetric cinematic lighting, premium dark cyberpunk-inspired aesthetic, absolutely no text, no logos, no watermarks, no extra people, no broken anatomy, perfect facial consistency throughout. Each phase keeps a distinct side of the frame completely clear and empty for website text overlays.

## 🛠️ Extraction & Web Integration Workflow

Once you generate the video or individual frames, integrate them into the portfolio:

1. **Format Conversion:** Compress the images/video for the web:
   * **Images:** Convert to `.webp` format at `1920x1080` resolution (aim for ~120KB per frame).
   * **Video:** Convert the 16:9 video to highly optimized `.mp4` and `.webm` (approx. 2-4MB total, 24fps).
2. **File Placement:** Save the files inside the `/public/assets/` directory of your portfolio as:
   * `hero_bg.webp`
   * `about_bg.webp`
   * `skills_bg.webp`
   * `projects_bg.webp`
   * `experience_bg.webp`
   * `drive_bg.webp`
   * `contact_bg.webp`
3. **Execution:** The React application is already pre-engineered in `/src/components/AvatarLayer.tsx` to handle these assets. It automatically reads these files and applies smooth ScrollTrigger transitions between them on scroll, utilizing a high-performance 2.5D layered crossfade architecture!
